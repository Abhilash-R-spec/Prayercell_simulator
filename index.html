What Scripture says about these struggles…

On sexual sin: "Flee from sexual immorality. Every other sin a person commits is outside the body, but the sexually immoral person sins against his own body." — 1 Corinthians 6:18. Fleeing means having brothers who know your struggle and hold you accountable.
On anger: "Everyone should be quick to listen, slow to speak and slow to become angry, because human anger does not produce the righteousness that God desires." — James 1:19-20
On pride: "God opposes the proud but shows favour to the humble." — James 4:6. The guys who pretend to have it together are the most at risk.
On substance use: "Do not get drunk on wine, which leads to debauchery. Instead, be filled with the Spirit." — Ephesians 5:18. The question is always: what are you trying to fill?
On confession: "Confess your sins to each other and pray for each other so that you may be healed." — James 5:16. This is what a brothers prayer cell is literally designed for.
